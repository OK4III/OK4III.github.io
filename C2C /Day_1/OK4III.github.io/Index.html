The Chronicles of a Hungry Rebel

Read time: 40 minutes

Hello, I'm writing this because at my very core I am a fat kid. I'm a professional eater since 2003. Kai's love affair with food began in her grandmother's kitchen, a place filled with the scents of freshly baked bread, simmering stews, and the warmth of family gatherings. As a child, she would spend hours watching her grandmother, a woman who believed that the best meals were made with love and shared with others. These early experiences planted the seeds of Kai's lifelong passion for food.

"big back, big back" - Kai, since 2003

Eating is not just about filling a need; it's an art form. She approaches every meal with a sense of reverence and excitement, whether she's dining at a Michelin-starred restaurant or enjoying a simple homemade soup. Her philosophy is that food should be enjoyed with all the senses—the sight of a beautifully presented dish, the aroma of fresh ingredients, the sound of a crispy crust being broken, the texture of food on the tongue, and, of course, the taste. “I take my time with every bite,” she says with a smile. “I want to fully experience each flavor, each texture. There's something magical about letting the flavors unfold slowly, really paying attention to what's happening in your mouth.”

"nom nom nom. slurp." - Kai, 2013

Kai recalls eating her favorite burger through a sonnet:

Beneath the flicker of the neon's glow, A greasy burger tempts the midnight air, Its golden bun, a soft and tender show, With juices running wild, beyond compare.

The cheese cascades, a molten, creamy stream, It clings to fingers, rich with savory grace, Each bite a guilty pleasure, like a dream, That paints a messy smile upon my face.

The patty's char, a smoky, seared embrace, In every mouthful, heaven's taste unfurled, Lettuce and onions find their fleeting place, In greasy splendor, all my woes are hurled.

Though health may scorn this feast of fleeting sin, In each indulgent bite, pure joy I win.

"my heart feels funny." - Kai, 2024
